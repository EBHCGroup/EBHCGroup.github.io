The Evidence-based Health Care Group is an educational collaborative that provides customized consultation to organizations interested in implementing evidence-based health care.

We offer a variety of services to assist in this process, including:

  • Needs Assessment
  • Training & Education
  • Curriculum Development
  • Implementation Strategies & Evaluation

Our mission is to help healthcare professionals and organizations build appropriate skills and infrastructure to implement EBHC and improve health outcomes.
